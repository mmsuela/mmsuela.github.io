With about four hours left until deadline, the news editors informed me that our original art plan had fallen through: we would not be able to obtain an image of the fires. I quickly began to work on an alternative.

Despite experiencing California wildfires, most SJSU students didn't understand the impact and scope of the Australian wildfires. The design is straight to the point - it shows all of Australia, on fire. Whitespace is used to ensure that no other content distracts from the illustration or headline quote. Rather than try to write a headline for the story, we let the students from Australia speak for themselves.

This front page received an honorable mention for best front page design in the 2020 CMA Pinnacle Awards
